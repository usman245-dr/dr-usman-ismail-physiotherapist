Dr. Usman Ismail DPT

A dedicated and compassionate Doctor of Physiotherapy committed to providing the highest quality of care to every patient.

Dr. Usman Ismail holds a Doctor of Physiotherapy (DPT) degree and has extensive experience in musculoskeletal rehabilitation, neurological physiotherapy, and pain management. With a patient-centered approach, he ensures personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual's needs.

Whether you visit the clinic or require treatment at home, Dr. Usman provides comprehensive physiotherapy services using modern techniques and equipment. His goal is to help patients regain mobility, reduce pain, and improve their quality of life.
